About Me

Thomas One is a Charlotte-based filmmaker with 3 years of professional Production experience from the Los Angeles film industry. Prior to his contributions in Los Angeles, his passion for filmmaking had taken him to work internationally as a director, editor, and event shooter (such as: music festivals, live performances, weddings) totaling 6 years of overall filmmaking experience. He's specialized in the Grip, Electric, and Production Departments from work on numerous feature films, television, and commercial productions.

Thomas loves collaboration with visionary individuals to deliver beautiful pictures with powerful and compelling stories. His filmmaking style is characterized by creative lighting, rhythmic music/sound editing, and kinetic camera movements.

Film & Photography Services

Thomas is based in Charlotte, North Carolina and is available for contract work as a Director, Director of Photography, Gaffer, Key Grip, and Portrait Photographer. He is able to work with clients from development to post-production.
